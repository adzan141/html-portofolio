1. Ousama Ranking

"Ousama Ranking" is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Sousuke Touka. It is also known as "Ranking of Kings" in English. The story revolves around Bojji, a young prince who is considered unfit to be king due to his deafness and perceived weakness. Despite this, he is determined to prove himself worthy of the throne and sets out on a journey accompanied by his loyal friend Kage, a shadow that can take on various forms. The series explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and the true meaning of strength. "Ousama Ranking" has received praise for its unique story, engaging characters, and emotional depth. It has been adapted into an anime series which premiered in October 2021.

2. "Attack on Titan" (Shingeki no Kyojin)

A dark fantasy anime set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the mysteries surrounding their existence.

3. "My Hero Academia" (Boku no Hero Academia)

- A superhero-themed anime set in a world where almost everyone possesses superpowers known as "Quirks." The series follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero, as he enrolls in a prestigious academy for aspiring heroes.

4. "Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood"

An epic fantasy anime set in a world where alchemy is practiced as a science. The series follows two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, as they search for the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies after a failed alchemical experiment.

5. "Spirited Away" (Sen to Chihiro no Kamikakushi)

A critically acclaimed animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. The story follows a young girl named Chihiro who becomes trapped in a mysterious world of spirits and must work in a bathhouse to save her parents and find her way back home.
